#### Test Bitmap Collision with Circle Using a Point and Bitmap Cell

Tests if a bitmap cell drawn using a passed in point and bitmap cell will intersect with a circle. You can use this to detect collisions between bitmaps and circles.

Uses the [bitmap_circle_collision](/api/physics/#bitmap-circle-collision-4) function.
