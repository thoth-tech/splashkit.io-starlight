## Description

Sky Surge is a top-down shooter which draws inspiration from classics such as Strikers 1945. In this game players take control of a powerful aircraft, navigating through challenging levels filled with waves of enemies, power-ups and intense boss battles. This twists conventional shooters through the incorporation of a unique mechanic which causes the player to grow in size and shoot in a larger radius, raising not only the stakes but increasing the sense of accomplishment for players through progression.

## Gameplay
        
In Sky Surge, players pilot a powerful aircraft in a top-down shooter experience reminiscent of classics like Strikers 1945. Navigate through challenging levels packed with enemy waves, power-ups, and intense boss battles. A unique mechanic sets this game apart, allowing your aircraft to grow in size and shoot in a larger radius as you progress. This feature not only intensifies the action but also amplifies the sense of accomplishment as you advance through the game.

| Action       | Player 1 |
|--------------|----------|
| Move Up      | W        |
| Move Down    | S        |
| Move Left    | A        |
| Move Right   | D        |
| Shoot        | Space    |
