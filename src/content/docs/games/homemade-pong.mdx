## Installation

1. Download the game files.
2. Navigate to the folder.
3. Execute `skm clang++ program.cpp paddle.cpp -o output` through the MSYS2 terminal.
4. Enter the command `./output` to play!

## Gameplay

In this homemade version of Pong, players compete in a fast-paced table tennis match. The objective is simple: use your paddle to hit the ball past your opponent's side to score points. Quick reflexes and precise timing are essential to outmaneuver your opponent and claim victory in this classic arcade showdown!

| Player        | Move Up   | Move Down   |
|---------------|-----------|-------------|
| **Player 1**  | W         | S           |
| **Player 2**  | Up Arrow  | Down Arrow  |
