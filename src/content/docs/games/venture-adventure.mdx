## Description

Venture Adventure is a thrilling puzzle game inspired by classic box-pushing mechanics. Navigate through challenging levels as you push boxes to collect gems while avoiding giant ants that threaten to end your adventure. Solve puzzles and strategize your moves to outwit the ants and gather all the gems to advance through each level.

## Installation

1. Download the game files.
1. Navigate to the folder.
1. Execute `skm clang++ boxes.cpp enemy.cpp game.cpp gems.cpp player.cpp program.cpp -o output` through the MSYS2 terminal.
1. Enter the command `./output` to play!

## Gameplay
        
In Venture Adventure, players tackle intricate puzzles by pushing boxes through a maze-like environment. The goal is to strategically position boxes to collect gems scattered throughout each level while avoiding giant ants that roam the area. Players must carefully plan their moves to solve each puzzle and outmaneuver the ants, ensuring they gather all the gems and progress to increasingly complex levels. Success requires a blend of logic, strategy, and quick reflexes.

| Action     | Player One Key | Player Two Key |
| ---------- | -------------- | -------------- |
| Move Up    | W              | UP_ARROW       |
| Move Left  | A              | LEFT_ARROW     |
| Move Down  | S              | DOWN_ARROW     |
| Move Right | D              | RIGHT_ARROW    |
